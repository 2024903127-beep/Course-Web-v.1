What It Is

A Standalone Healthcare Point — No Hospital Required

An ArogyaX health booth is a compact, touchscreen-enabled kiosk that connects patients with a doctor through a structured consultation workflow. It doesn't need dedicated medical staff on-site to operate.

The booth handles patient registration, guides the user through vitals capture, submits the case to a connected doctor, and delivers a prescription or follow-up instruction — all in a single session.

Each booth is pre-configured for the institution before deployment. It runs on the ArogyaX platform, meaning all data flows into the same secure backend used by web and mobile users.

Booth Capabilities

What Happens Inside a Consultation Session

👤

Patient Registration

New or returning patients register via OTP verification or guest access, depending on the institution's configuration. No paperwork, no forms to fill by hand.

💓

Vitals Capture

The booth guides the patient through measuring available vitals — including blood pressure, pulse, temperature, and SpO2 — using connected peripherals or manual entry.

🩺

Doctor Connection

The consultation request is submitted to an available doctor in the connected queue. The doctor reviews vitals and conducts the session over audio, video, or text as configured.

📝

Symptom Logging

Patients describe their concern through a structured, guided interface. The system formats this as a case summary for the reviewing doctor, reducing back-and-forth.

📄

Prescription Output

Doctor issues a digitally signed prescription. The patient receives it on screen for review, and can have it sent via SMS or collected as a printed copy if a printer is attached.

📊

Session Records

Every session is logged with a timestamp, session ID, and audit trail. Institutions access aggregate data and individual records through the admin dashboard.

Configurations

Flexible by Design — Set Up for Your Policy

Every institution is different. ArogyaX booths ship with configuration options that let institutions set the rules that match their requirements.

OTP Verification — On

Patients verify their identity via a one-time password sent to their registered mobile number. Consultations are tied to a named patient profile. Recommended for hospitals and clinics where records continuity matters.

  • Patient profile linked to every session
  • Consultation history available across visits
  • Prescription tied to verified identity

OTP Verification — Off (Guest Mode)

Patients consult without creating an account or sharing identity. The session is anonymous. Suitable for public health booths, corporate settings with privacy-first HR policies, or open community deployments.

  • No registration friction for the patient
  • Prescription generated and shared for that session only
  • Audit logs maintained at the system level (non-identifiable)

Paid Consultation — Enabled

Patients pay a configured fee per consultation. Supports UPI, card, and institutional billing integrations. Suitable for clinics, pharmacies, and private hospital OPDs operating on a revenue model.

  • Per-session or subscription fee models
  • Digital payment with receipt generation
  • Revenue tracked in admin dashboard

Paid Consultation — Free Mode

All consultations are provided at no cost to the patient. Suitable for school health programmes, government deployments, and employer-sponsored employee wellness setups where the institution covers the cost.

  • No payment friction — patients access care instantly
  • Institutional billing handled separately
  • Useful for CSR and public health mandates
