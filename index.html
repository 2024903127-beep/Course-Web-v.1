How It Works

From Registration to Prescription in Minutes

A seamless, guided workflow that works across all ArogyaX deployment types — booths, web, or mobile.

1

Patient Registration

Patient registers via OTP or guest mode — at a booth, on the web, or through the mobile app.

2

Vitals Capture

Basic health data is recorded through kiosk sensors or entered manually by a healthcare aide.

3

Doctor Review

Case enters the doctor's queue. Physician reviews vitals, history, and conducts consultation.

4

Prescription & Followup

Doctor issues a secure digital prescription. Patient receives a PDF or summary via the platform.
