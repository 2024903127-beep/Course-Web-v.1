We've compiled answers to the most common questions our students ask. If you can't find what you're looking for, don't hesitate to contact our support team.

Getting Started

Do I need any prior trading experience to join your courses?

+

No prior trading experience is required! Our courses are designed for complete beginners. We start with the fundamentals and build up to advanced strategies. Many of our most successful students started with zero trading knowledge.

How much time do I need to dedicate to learning trading?

+

We recommend dedicating 5-10 hours per week for optimal learning. Our courses are flexible - you can learn at your own pace. The key is consistency rather than cramming all at once.

Is trading education legal in India?

+

Yes, trading education is completely legal in India. We provide educational content only and do not offer investment advice or manage funds. Our courses teach trading strategies and market analysis skills.

Can I trade while learning?

+

We strongly recommend learning first before trading with real money. Use paper trading (simulated trading) to practice strategies without financial risk. Only start live trading once you're consistently profitable in simulation.

Trading Questions

Do you guarantee profits from trading?

+

No trading education provider can guarantee profits. Trading involves significant risk and past performance doesn't guarantee future results. We teach you proven strategies and risk management, but success depends on your dedication, discipline, and market conditions.

What trading strategies do you teach?

+

We teach multiple proven strategies including: Technical Analysis, Price Action Trading, Support & Resistance, Trend Following, Breakout Trading, Risk Management, and Position Sizing. We focus on strategies that work in Indian markets.

Do you recommend specific brokers or trading platforms?

+

We don't endorse specific brokers, but we recommend using SEBI-registered brokers with good reputations. We teach platform-agnostic strategies that work on most trading platforms including Zerodha, Upstox, Angel One, and others.

How much capital do I need to start trading?

+

You can start with as little as ₹5,000-10,000 for equity trading. However, we recommend starting with ₹25,000+ for better risk management and position sizing. The key is not how much you start with, but how well you manage risk.

Technical Support

I'm having trouble accessing my account. What should I do?

+

First, try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" link. If that doesn't work, clear your browser cache and cookies, or try a different browser. If issues persist, contact our support team with your email address.

Videos are not playing. How can I fix this?

+

Try refreshing the page, clearing your browser cache, or using a different browser. Ensure you have a stable internet connection. If using mobile, check your data/WiFi connection. Contact support if the issue continues.

Which browsers do you support?

+

Our platform works best with modern browsers: Chrome (recommended), Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience.

Can I download course videos for offline viewing?

+

Currently, videos must be viewed online due to copyright and licensing restrictions. However, you can access all course materials from any device with an internet connection.
