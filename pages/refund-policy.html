Refund Policy

Our commitment to your satisfaction and learning success.

Our Refund Guarantee

At Rahul Mishra Trading Academy, we are committed to providing high-quality trading education that delivers real value to our students. We understand that learning to trade is a significant investment, and we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase.

Our refund policy is designed to protect both students and ensure the integrity of our educational programs. We offer a comprehensive refund guarantee with clear terms and conditions.

Course Refund Policy

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all our trading courses. If you're not completely satisfied with the course content, quality, or your learning experience within the first 30 days of purchase, you may request a full refund.

Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for a refund, you must:

  • Request the refund within 30 days of purchase
  • Have completed less than 50% of the course content
  • Provide specific feedback about why you're unsatisfied
  • Not have violated our terms of service

Refund Processing

Once your refund request is approved:

  • Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days
  • You will receive the refund via your original payment method
  • Access to course materials will be revoked immediately
  • Any bonuses or additional materials will be forfeited

Mentorship & Premium Programs

For our premium mentorship programs and one-on-one coaching:

  • 14-day money-back guarantee for mentorship packages
  • 7-day money-back guarantee for one-on-one coaching sessions
  • Refunds available only if no sessions have been completed
  • Custom program refunds evaluated on a case-by-case basis

Non-Refundable Items

The following purchases are not eligible for refunds:

  • Digital downloads and resources after 7 days
  • Live trading signals and alerts
  • Third-party tools and software licenses
  • Physical merchandise and printed materials
  • Items purchased during promotional sales (final sale)

Special Circumstances

We understand that life circumstances can change. In special cases, we may consider refund requests beyond our standard policy:

  • Medical emergencies or serious illness
  • Significant life changes affecting study ability
  • Technical issues preventing course access (our fault)
  • Course content significantly different from advertised

All special circumstance requests are reviewed individually by our customer service team.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Contact our customer support team
  2. Provide your order number and reason for refund
  3. Include any relevant documentation if applicable
  4. Allow 2-3 business days for review
