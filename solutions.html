Solutions

The Right Fit for Every Healthcare Setting

ArogyaX is purpose-built for a range of institutions — each with unique workflows, patient volumes, and infrastructure constraints. Here's how we address them.

🏥 Hospitals

Managing High Patient Volume Without Losing Quality

The Problem

Hospital OPDs are overwhelmed. Doctors spend time on intake and triage that could be automated. Rural branches often operate without adequate specialist coverage.

  • Deploy health booths at entry points for pre-consultation vitals capture
  • Auto-route patients to appropriate doctor queues
  • Enable remote specialist consultations from within the same platform
  • Generate prescriptions without paper-based workflows
  • Centralised admin dashboard for all branches and departments

🩺 Clinics

Lightweight Consultation Stack for Independent Practices

The Problem

Small clinics can't afford complex HMS software. They need a simple, reliable way to manage consultations and maintain records without a full IT team.

  • Web-based consultation flow — no booth hardware required
  • Per-consultation or subscription billing model
  • Simple doctor onboarding — live in under an hour
  • Branded prescription PDFs with clinic details
  • Patient history accessible across sessions
Request Clinic Demo
🎓 Schools

Student Health Programs That Actually Work at Scale

The Problem

School health programs are reactive, not proactive. Screening events are episodic and records are rarely maintained. There's no infrastructure for follow-up care.

  • Health booths placed in school premises for ongoing access
  • Student profile management with year-on-year health tracking
  • Consent-based data handling in line with child privacy norms
  • Lightweight doctor review for flagged cases
  • Reports exportable for administration and parents

🏢 Corporate Offices

Employee Wellness Infrastructure That Shows Results

The Problem

Corporate wellness programmes often amount to a gym membership and an annual health camp. Employees have no easy way to consult a doctor during the workday.

  • Booths placed in office common areas for quick access
  • Anonymous or identified consultations based on HR policy
  • Integration with employee health benefit systems
  • Aggregated (non-identifiable) health reports for HR teams
  • Follow-up reminder workflows built into the platform

🌐 Public Health Programmes

Scalable Infrastructure for Government and NGO Health Initiatives

The Problem

Public health programmes lack the technology to reach underserved populations at scale. Point-of-care solutions are too expensive and too slow to deploy.

  • Booths deployable in PHCs, community centres, and panchayat offices
  • Offline-capable kiosk operation for low-connectivity environments
  • Multilingual interface support for diverse populations
  • Aggregate reporting dashboards for programme monitoring
  • Flexible pricing for government and NGO procurement
